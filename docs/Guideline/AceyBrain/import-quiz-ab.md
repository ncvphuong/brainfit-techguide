# Importing Quiz and Publishing Image on BrainFit Studio Platform
This feature is for SA

## Steps to Import a Quiz

1. Navigate to [BrainFit Studio ACP](https://acp.brainfitstudio.com/acp/thinkworkout/).  
2. Click **"Quiz"**.  

![Image](./img/import-quiz-ab-1.png)


3. Click **"IMPORT"**. 

![Image](./img/import-quiz-ab-2.png)


4. Click the **file field** to upload the import file.  

![Image](./img/import-quiz-ab-3.png)


5. Click **"Save"**.  

![Image](./img/import-quiz-ab-4.png)



## Steps to Publish an Image for a Quiz

6. Click **"Quiz"** again. 

![Image](./img/import-quiz-ab-5.png)


7. Click **here**.  

![Image](./img/import-quiz-ab-6.png)


8. Click **"Upload File"** to upload an image for the quiz.

![Image](./img/import-quiz-ab-7.png)


9. Click **here**.  

![Image](./img/import-quiz-ab-8.png)


10. Click **"Publish"**.  

![Image](./img/import-quiz-ab-9.png)


11. The **upload history** will be displayed in the list below.  

![Image](./img/import-quiz-ab-10.png)
