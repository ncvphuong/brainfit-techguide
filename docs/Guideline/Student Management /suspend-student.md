# Suspend Student
1. Navigate to [ACP Brainfit Studio](https://acp.brainfitstudio.com/acp/)
2. Click **"Students"**

![Image](./img/suspend_student_1.png)

3. Type the name of the student you want to suspend

![Image](./img/suspend_student_2.png)

4. Click **"Search"**

![Image](./img/suspend_student_3.png)


5. Click **here** to suspend

![Image](./img/suspend_student_4.png)


6. Click **OK** to confirm

7. Click **here** to watch change logs

![Image](./img/suspend_student_5.png)


8. Change logs will be listed **here**

![Image](./img/suspend_student_6.png)


9. Click this button to view **details**

![Image](./img/suspend_student_7.png)


10. Click **here** to unsuspend

![Image](./img/suspend_student_9.png)


11. Change logs will be **saved**

![Image](./img/suspend_student_10.png)

![Image](./img/suspend_student_11.png)
