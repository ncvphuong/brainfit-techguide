# How to Publish Progress Reports in Student Detail View

## Steps to Search for a Student's Progress Report

1. Navigate to [BrainFit ACP](https://acp.brainfitstudio.com/acp/).  
2. Click **"Students"**.  

![Image](./img/publish-progress-report-1-student-detail.png)


3. Click the **"Name..."** field.  

![Image](./img/publish-progress-report-2-student-detail.png)


4. Type the **student's name**.  
5. Click **"Search"**.  

![Image](./img/publish-progress-report-3-student-detail.png)


6. Click **here**.  

![Image](./img/publish-progress-report-4-student-detail.png)



## Steps to Publish a Progress Report  

7. Click **"Publish"**.  

![Image](./img/publish-progress-report-5-student-detail.png)


8. Click **here** and choose a file.  

![Image](./img/publish-progress-report-6-student-detail.png)


9. Click **"Publish"** again.  

![Image](./img/publish-progress-report-7-student-detail.png)
