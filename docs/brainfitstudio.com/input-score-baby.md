# Input Scores for Brainfit Baby Game

1. Navigate to [ACP Brainfit Studio](https://acp.brainfitstudio.com/acp).  
2. Click **"Systems"**.  

![Image](./img/input-score-baby-1.png)


3. Click **"Input Scores"**.  

![Image](./img/input-score-baby-2.png)


4. Click **"Brainfit Babies"**. 

![Image](./img/input-score-baby-3.png)


5. Click **"Classes"**.  

![Image](./img/input-score-baby-4.png)


6. Click the **"Class"** field to choose a class.  

![Image](./img/input-score-baby-5.png)


7. Click the **"Game"** field to choose a subgame.  

![Image](./img/input-score-baby-8.png)


8. Click **"Search"**.  

![Image](./img/input-score-baby-6.png)


9. Click **"Input"**. 

![Image](./img/input-score-baby-7.png)
