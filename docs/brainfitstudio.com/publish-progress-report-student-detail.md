1. Navigate to [ACP Portal](https://acp.brainfitstudio.com/acp/).  
2. Click **"Students"**.  

![Image](./img/publish-progress-report-1.png)


3. Click the **"Name..."** field.  

![Image](./img/publish-progress-report-2.png)


4. Type the **student's name**.  
5. Click **"Search"**.  

![Image](./img/publish-progress-report-3.png)


6. Click **here**.
