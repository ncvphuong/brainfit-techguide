# How to Publish Individual Assignments in ACP

1. Navigate to [ACP Brainfit Studio](https://acp.brainfitstudio.com/acp/).  
2. Click **"Course"**.  

![Image](./img/publish-individual-course-1.png)

3. Click **"Course Individual Assignment"**.  

![Image](./img/publish-individual-course-2.png)


4. Type the **name of the student**.  

![Image](./img/publish-individual-course-3.png)


5. Click **"Search"**.  

![Image](./img/publish-individual-course-4.png)


6. Click **"Assign"**.  

![Image](./img/publish-individual-course-5.png)


7. Click **"Assign Courses"**.  

![Image](./img/publish-individual-course-6.png)


8. Click **here**.  

![Image](./img/publish-individual-course-7.png)


9. Select the **Course**.  
10. Click **"Submit"**.  

![Image](./img/publish-individual-course-8.png)


11. Click **"Individual Publish Date"** to schedule the publish date.  

![Image](./img/publish-individual-course-9.png)
