# BrainFit Singapore

**Cognitive Fitness for Learning Excellence**

Founded in 2001, **BrainFit® Singapore** is a neuroscience-based training center dedicated to enhancing cognitive and learning skills for children and youth. With over two decades of research and hands-on experience, BrainFit delivers brain-based programs that help individuals maximize their learning potential.

![BrainFit Logo](https://brainfit.com.sg/wp-content/uploads/2020/01/BrainFit-Logo.png)

## 🚀 Mission

BrainFit® aims to strengthen brain networks responsible for attention, memory, processing speed, and emotional regulation — the foundations for effective learning.

The programs are grounded in neuroscience and personalized to each learner, enabling better focus, academic performance, and long-term success.

## 🧠 Core Programs

### 1. **BrainFit® Baby**  
*For children aged 9–36 months*

Early neurodevelopmental training to build sensory-motor and language foundations through play-based, parent-guided sessions.

### 2. **BrainFit® Junior**  
*For children aged 3–6 years*

Designed to improve attention, auditory and visual processing, memory, and confidence to prepare for primary school success.

### 3. **BrainFit® Scholar**  
*For children aged 7 and up*

A comprehensive brain training program focused on strengthening five key cognitive pillars: attention, memory, processing speed, language, and social-emotional regulation.

## 🧪 Scientific Approach

- Grounded in **neuroscience research**
- Tools and methods adapted from top research institutions
- Programs track and measure progress over time
- Personalized coaching by trained professionals
